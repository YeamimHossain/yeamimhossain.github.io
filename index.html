Bio

Md Yeamim Hossain earned his B.S. in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh in 2019. During his undergraduate studies, he developed strong academic and research interests in machine learning, data science, signal processing, biomedical signal analysis, and deep learning. His undergraduate thesis focused on the application of machine learning techniques to biomedical signal processing, where he worked with signals such as EEG, EMG, and ECG for classification and analysis tasks. This research experience resulted in several publications in IEEE conferences, strengthening his foundation in applied machine learning research.

To further expand his expertise, he is currently pursuing an M.S. in Information Technology at Jahangirnagar University, where his coursework includes machine learning, artificial intelligence, data science, IoT, cloud computing, human-computer interaction, and data mining. His graduate studies have broadened his knowledge in intelligent systems and advanced computational technologies, reinforcing his long-term goal of contributing to innovative research in machine learning and related interdisciplinary domains.

In addition to his academic background, he has developed practical proficiency in programming languages, machine learning frameworks, simulation software, networking tools, and system design platforms, enabling him to bridge theoretical knowledge with hands-on implementation. He also served as a Teaching Assistant in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh, where he assisted in laboratory instruction, prepared course materials, maintained academic documentation, and supported students through academic guidance and mentoring. This role enhanced his communication, organizational, and instructional skills while strengthening his commitment to academic excellence.

Beyond academics, Yeamim is passionate about research, project development, coding, and design, and actively participated in technical competitions and project-based activities throughout his university years. In his leisure time, he enjoys reading novels, watching films, playing video games, traveling, and spending quality time with his family. With a strong academic foundation, research experience, and a growing specialization in machine learning, he aspires to pursue advanced research and build a meaningful career in artificial intelligence and intelligent systems.

Research Interest: Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Bioinformatics, Computer Vision, Federated Learning (FL), Convex Optimization, Signal Processing, Wireless Communication.
