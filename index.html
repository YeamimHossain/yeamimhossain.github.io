Bio

I am a prospective master/PhD student. I completed my B.Sc. in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE) at the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB), Dhaka, Bangladesh in summer 2019. During my B.Sc. thesis, I was supervised by Abul Barkat Mollah Sayeed Ud Doulah, PhD.

My research interests focus on machine learning, signal processing, biomedical signal analysis, deep learning, wireless communication and also embedded system. During my undergrad thesis, I gained knowledge in the field of machine learning, which drove my desire to publish conference proceedings in this area. I had worked on the detection of motor imagery EEG signals using machine learning techniques and also do a comparative analysis to find out the better performance using different ML algorithms. Both of these research works were published by the IEEE conferences. I am motivated to build my career in this field and develop myself by gathering knowledge and practical experience.

Throughout my undergraduate studies, I honed my abilities in different programming languages (such as MATLAB, Python, and C programming), software and hardware skills, embedded systems (based on Arduino), etc. On the basis of my experiences and skills, I was appointed as Teaching Assistant (TA) at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), ULAB, Bangladesh. As a TA, my duties and responsibilities were to assist the faculty to conduct the lab classes, assist the faculty in preparing course materials, assist the faculty in maintaining course-related documents (lab report, assignment, document scrutinization), counsel students within my office hours, and invigilation duties sometimes.

My hobby is reading novels, watching movies, playing video games, and traveling. Besides, I love to do research, build projects, coding and also designing. As a consequence, I actively participate in different contests and projects showcasing in my university life. Basically, I like to explore and experience new things. Above of all, I love to spend time with my family.
